Art Hub Signage
Illustration, Signage, Typography

Signage designs for a University Art Hub. The Art Hub is a space for creatives to relax or work on their projects.

Top photo is an exterior design for the outside of a door. Graphics are arranged to depict the different activities that students may utilize the room for. Bottom photo is an interior design for a poster to show students the rules of utilizing the art hub. The creative approach was to characterize the objects in the graphics by giving them facial attributes. In addition, solid shapes and lines in various shades or tints of the same color palette are used to unite the interior and exterior spaces while keeping the signages visible from far distances.
