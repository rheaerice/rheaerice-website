Let's Dive Into Paradise
Electronic Book Publication

An informational book for a person who is visiting Hawaii. It features Hawaii's geographical traits and activities.

The design is made to guide the reader through each page as one would when they explore Hawaii. With every slide, the perspective of the viewer changes, giving hint to the next setting of the journey. To create a dynamic that engages with the audience, interactive buttons and animations are placed within multiple pages. In addition, the artistic approach of using playful colors matched with simple graphics are chosen as a fun way to informally educate readers about the many aspects of the island.
