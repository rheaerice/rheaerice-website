How to Cook an Egg Sunny–Side Up
Infographic, Isometric Grid, Illustration

A school project to show how to cook an egg sunny–side up in less than ten steps and without depending on text for description.

The challenge was to use an isometric grid as a base for all illustrations while using a limited amount of three colors. To highlight the vibrant color of an egg yolk, the design was made with complimentary colors and a neutral color to allow for smaller details. Composition follows an “S” shaped sequence to visually guide audiences through each step in the correct order.
