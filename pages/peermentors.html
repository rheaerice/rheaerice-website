Peer Mentor Services
“Be The Next Generation”
Visual Identity, Marketing, Recruitment Campaign

Peer Mentor Services is an organization at California State University, East Bay that helps first year and transfer students become successful learners by providing resourceful mentors both inside and outside the classroom. “Be The Next Generation” is the recruitment campaign to encourage current students to apply and become the next generation of peer mentors for the following school year.

The goal for the program's advertisement products was to build a consistent brand and slogan that matches the existing logo. By utilizing the University's colors and style guide along with the program's customized logo, the presence of the organization was well established around campus through different forms of promotional materials. Designs are altered to accomodate varying sizes of posters and flyers that provide information towards application workshops and traits of the program.
