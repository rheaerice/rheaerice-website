Sleep On It Educational Brochure
Infographic Design, Illustration, Print

Design concept for an educational brochure which communicates the idea that one of the main causes of not getting enough sleep is the over-usage of technology at night.
My brochure design would allow people a quick and simple way to become educated of their negative sleeping habits and find out how they can improve it. Instead of technology being a negative influence on sleep, this brochure is to encourage teenagers to use their electronic devices to help them develop healthier sleeping patterns. One of the ways this brochure will accomplish that is to offer phone apps and tips to track their sleeping schedules.
