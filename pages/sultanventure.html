Sultan Ventures
Marketing, Branding, GIFs

Sultan Ventures is a venture firm helping small businesses accelerate growth by providing resources through a network of experts and investors. Designs include digital and print promotional flyers for their workshops and GIFs to engage with audiences.

The GIFs are created using the Sultan Ventures logo and their Accelerator and Incubator program logos. The goal was to stimulate the logos in spite of being composed of geometric characters. Therefore, focus was given to developing playful motions that captivate the eyes in a timely manner. These GIFs are available to use on social media platforms through GIPHY.


Here are two design solutions for a workshop with Sultan Venture's Accelerator, XLR8HI. One of the challenges was to create a neutral and professional flyer that appeals to an all gender–based entrepreneur audience.
