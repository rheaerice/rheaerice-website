Sundae Scoop Ice Cream
Brand Identity, Packaging, Logo Design

A school project to create a company of choice. This is a concept for an ice cream company, Sundae Scoop. Included is a brand style guide and packaging mock–ups for their products.

Inspired by classic ice cream flavors, the colors evoke a nostalgia for vibrantly filled childhood days. The logo design and typeface choices follow the round and curvy motions found within the company's name.
