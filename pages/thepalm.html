The Palm Restaurant Menu
Visual Identity, Logo Design, Illustration

Design concept for a beach–side restaurant, The Palm .
The Palm is inspired by a modern style with a tropical color palette.

To achieve the minimalistic aesthetic of a clean and casual restaurant, the design is made with subtle colors and few graphics. The generous amount of negative space allows readers to focus on the text content of the menu and appreciate the unique selection of dishes offered.
