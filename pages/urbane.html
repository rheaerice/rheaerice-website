Urbane Kitchen Supplies
Branding, Packaging, Illustration

Design concept for a kitchen supplies company, Urbane.
Urbane encourages cooking at home by giving people the confidence to be in the kitchen through design that is modern and casual. Urbane is inspired by the idea of cooking as a form of art.

The name “Urbane”, is derived from mid–16th century French or Latin terms to describe a person who is refined in manner or is sophisticated. Illustrations are hand drawn on Photoshop using pencil and paint brushes and printed on recycled craft paper. The nostalgia of traditional art is expressed while maintaining a contemporary look through use of sans-serif typeface.
