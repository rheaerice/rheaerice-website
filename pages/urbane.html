Urbane Kitchen Supplies
Branding, Packaging, Illustration

Design concept for a kitchen supplies company, Urbane.
Urbane Kitchen Supplies encourages cooking at home by giving people the confidence to be in the kitchen through design that is modern and casual. Urbane Kitchen Supplies is inspired by the idea of cooking as a form of art.

The name “Urbane”, is derived from mid 16th century French or Latin terms to describe a person who is refined in manner or is sophisticated. Art during this time was also a way that elites showed off their power and status.
